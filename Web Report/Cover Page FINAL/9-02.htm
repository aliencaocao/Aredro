Project Title Aredro
Synopsis

Aredro is an ordering app that mainly focusses on ordering items from Aphelion Council
and let the students to submit their orders. Our aim for this app is to improve
the productivity and efficiency of ordering for both students and councillors.

We found that logging into iEMB, checking for the available items, and sumbitting an
order will take at least 2 minutes (including searching for the message and thinking what
to order). This app can help students find the available items and orders within a minute.
There is also functions for the councillors.The councillors can add, remove (available
items for order), check and delete (student orders) orders.
